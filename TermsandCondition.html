Rules of the Quiz

  1. 1. Only team entries are eligible.
  2. 2.A team shall consist of max two persons
  3. 3.The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.

  5. 5.The questions shall be in the form of multiple choice, True / False statement, Specific-answer question
